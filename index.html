Welcome to STRATUS

Storm Tracking, Research, And Targeted U.S. Studies — Advancing severe weather monitoring and atmospheric research.

Deployment Objectives

  • Offer a set of deployment-ready facilities and instruments that are available on request to support NSF research programs.
  • Maintain a technical and administrative staff sufficient to meet normal deployment levels and assist users installing equipment on deployed facilities.
  • Deploy and operate in diverse locations across the Earth as required by research, supporting logistical and technical aspects of complex field projects.
  • Assist and advise investigators as they develop plans and proposals and conduct supported research projects.
  • Organize expert evaluation of proposed field projects and perform administrative functions, including documentation of results and compliance with regulations.
  • Cooperate with other agencies and facilities to provide collaborative support to the broad atmospheric-research community.

Agriculture Applications

STRATUS supports farmers and researchers by providing real-time weather insights, soil moisture tracking, and forecast tools for sustainable crop management.

About Us

STRATUS is a research initiative dedicated to atmospheric data collection, storm tracking, and field deployment studies across the United States.
