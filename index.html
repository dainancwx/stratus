Welcome to STRATUS

STRATUS (Storm Tracking, Research, And Targeted U.S. Studies) is an atmospheric research and field deployment program focused on advancing severe weather observation, forecasting, and environmental analysis across the United States.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between meteorological science and real-world applications by developing field-ready technologies and providing open-access data for researchers, emergency management, and agricultural operations. STRATUS teams operate advanced sensor arrays, deploy mobile weather platforms, and analyze atmospheric data to better understand storm dynamics, wind structure, and local climate effects.
