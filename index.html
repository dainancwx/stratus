Welcome to STRATUS

Storm Tracking, Research, And Targeted U.S. Studies — Advancing severe weather monitoring and atmospheric research.

Deployment Objectives

  • Conduct hands-on field research to gather critical atmospheric and storm data across diverse U.S. regions.
  • Maintain a technical and administrative staff sufficient to meet normal deployment levels and assist users installing equipment on deployed facilities.
  • Provide researchers and partners with advanced instrumentation and support for real-time data collection.
  • Enable safe and efficient storm tracking missions while prioritizing team safety and operational excellence.
  • Collaborate with academic and governmental institutions to advance severe weather monitoring techniques.
  • Document and share field observations to support forecasting, climate studies, and scientific publications.
  • Continuously refine deployment strategies to maximize data quality and research impact.

Agriculture Applications

STRATUS supports farmers and researchers by providing real-time weather insights, soil moisture tracking, and forecast tools for sustainable crop management.

About Us

STRATUS is a research initiative dedicated to atmospheric data collection, storm tracking, and field deployment studies across the United States.
