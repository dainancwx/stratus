About STRATUS

STRATUS (Storm Tracking, Research, And Targeted U.S. Studies) is a collaborative program focused on improving our understanding of severe weather and its impact on the environment and communities across the United States.

Our initiative combines the efforts of researchers, students, and atmospheric scientists to collect, analyze, and share meteorological data. We focus on mobile operations, real-time forecasting, and developing advanced tools to support emergency management and agricultural decision-making.

Through continuous collaboration with universities, government agencies, and private institutions, STRATUS strives to advance atmospheric science while fostering innovation in environmental technology.

For partnership inquiries or data access requests, please contact us at: info@stratus-research.org.
