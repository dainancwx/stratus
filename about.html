About STRATUS

STRATUS (Storm Tracking, Research, And Targeted U.S. Studies) is a multi-disciplinary program dedicated to the advancement of atmospheric and environmental sciences.

Our teams conduct field operations, develop research-grade instruments, and partner with universities and agencies to improve severe weather forecasting and public safety.
