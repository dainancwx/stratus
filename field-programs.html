Field Programs

STRATUS conducts field deployments to collect atmospheric data, test new instrumentation, and improve severe weather forecasting.

  • Operate in diverse U.S. regions for storm and boundary layer studies.
  • Support research teams with mobile sensor networks and radar coordination.
  • Deploy instrumentation for temperature, pressure, humidity, and wind profiling.
