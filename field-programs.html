Field Programs

STRATUS field programs are at the core of our mission — deploying advanced weather instrumentation and research teams to collect real-world data on storm systems, boundary layers, and atmospheric structure.

  • Conduct hands-on field research across diverse U.S. regions.
  • Maintain technical and administrative staff to support complex deployments.
  • Deploy advanced sensor networks for real-time data collection and analysis.
  • Collaborate with universities, agencies, and research teams for integrated field operations.
  • Focus on safe, efficient, and high-impact missions for scientific advancement.
