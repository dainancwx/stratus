Agriculture Applications

STRATUS supports agricultural innovation through precise environmental monitoring and forecasting systems. By integrating sensor data and meteorological modeling, our research aims to improve productivity, resilience, and sustainability for U.S. farmers.

  • Monitor soil moisture and temperature with high-resolution field sensors.
  • Track precipitation, evapotranspiration, and crop-impacting weather events.
  • Support farmers with accurate, location-based forecasts for decision-making.
  • Partner with agricultural researchers to study microclimate effects on yields.

STRATUS continues to expand its agricultural research network, offering tools for both small-scale and industrial operations seeking climate-smart solutions.
